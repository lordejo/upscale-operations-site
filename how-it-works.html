The process

Five steps to your team.

Most of it is on us. Here’s exactly how it goes from first conversation to a team that’s up and running.

1
Your part

Tell us what you need

Hop on a quick discovery call and walk us through your business, your bottlenecks, and the work you want off your plate. No formal brief required — a conversation is enough for us to get started.

2
Our part

We build your team

We match specialists from our roster whose skills fit exactly what you described — an executive assistant, a designer, a bookkeeper, whoever the work calls for — and assign a dedicated operations manager to run it all.

3
Your part

Choose your hours

Pick how many hours a month you need. They’re shared across your whole team, so you can flex them toward whatever the work is that week — heavy on design now, heavy on admin later.

4
Your part

Start your retainer

Kick off your plan with a simple monthly retainer — one predictable cost for the entire team. No per-person contracts, no setup fees, no surprise invoices.

5
Together

Onboard and go

We get access to your tools, learn your preferences, and your operations manager kicks things off. From here you delegate once — and it gets handled.
